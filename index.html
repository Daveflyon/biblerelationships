Bible Pointers on Marriage

A ten-lesson guide

The Well Shrewsbury

This Bible study guide has been created for the congregation of The Well Shrewsbury. It is designed for anyone thinking about marriage or already in one — single or married, new believer or well-established in faith. The series begins with foundations and builds through to the practical realities of everyday married life.

Each lesson goes deep on one question so that the truth has time to take root. Here is how each lesson is structured:

  • Opening Question — Answer this honestly in your own words before reading anything else. No right or wrong answer.
  • Key Scripture — The anchor verse for the lesson. Read it slowly before moving on.
  • Core Truth — The central idea of the lesson in one statement. Read it after the scripture and return to it at the end.
  • Bible Teaching — The biblical truth unpacked clearly, with every key point rooted in scripture. Four types of callout box appear throughout: Note (foundational explanation), Depth (going further), Tip (practical application) and Caution (common misunderstanding to avoid).
  • Key Discovery Questions — Work through these on your own before your group meets. Your answers are saved in your browser and can be downloaded as a text file.
  • Group Discussion Questions — Designed for open conversation at any level of experience.
  • Application — Practical ways to live out what the lesson teaches, addressed to both single and married people.
  • Revision Cards — Tap each card to test your recall of the key terms and scriptures.

Who this guide is not for

This series is not designed for someone in an immediate pastoral or personal crisis who needs one-to-one care before engaging with group study. It is not a replacement for pastoral support or professional help. If you are experiencing something that requires urgent support, please speak with your church leaders first.

The approach throughout is accessible and pastoral rather than academically scholarly. Where sincere Christians hold genuinely different views, this is acknowledged honestly, and you are directed to your church leaders for further conversation.

This study is based on teaching from the Discipleship Evangelism Programme (Andrew Wommack and Don W. Krow, delessons.org), the Marriage and Family series (Curtis Phillips, Charis Bible College) and the AWM Family Series (Andrew Wommack Ministries). Used with permission for discipleship purposes, free of charge. All scripture quotations are from the New King James Version (NKJV) unless otherwise indicated.
